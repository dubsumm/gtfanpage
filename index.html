Daft Punk

Bio

The French 2 man duo that changed the game for electronic music behind thier iconic robot helmets. Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo banded
together in 1993 producing hit after hit such as 'Robot Rock', 'Digital Love', and even had a #1 hit on a collab with the Weeknd on the song 'Starboy.'
If you ever have a couple hours to kill, I highly recommend checking out their album 'Discovery' which was animated into the short film "Interstella 5555" by the legenadry Leiji Matsumoto
